How it works

Three months.
No surprises.

You'll always know which step we're on, what's being built, and what the numbers say. Here's exactly how the three months go.

Month 01

Build

We start by learning your business — who your customers are and what a new customer is worth to you. Once we have everything we need from you, we get to work. Your website and full marketing package is built and live within the first month.

The timeline depends on you sending us what we need — the faster we have it, the faster we move. Either way, by the end of month one you have a website that people can find and trust, ready for everything that follows.

Month 02

Grow

Your ads go live. We send people from the ads to a simple page that's built to get them to take action. Then we start learning what works.

This is where we test — the words, the offer, the images. You get your first weekly reports and watch the numbers settle.

Month 03

Fine Tune

We keep what's working and cut what isn't. By now things are running smoothly — steady traffic, a page that converts, and numbers you can trust.

Then we hand it all over to you. Full access, a clear picture of what's running, and a system that keeps working — whether or not you continue with us.

Every week, one simple report. What we did, what it cost, and what it brought in. No confusing words. No hiding.
