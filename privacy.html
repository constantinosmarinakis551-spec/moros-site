Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

moros ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we handle it. By using our website or submitting our contact form, you agree to this policy.

1. Who We Are

moros is a digital marketing agency. Our contact email is hello@moros.com. For any questions about this policy, please reach out there.

2. What We Collect

We only collect information you give us directly through our contact form:

  • Your name
  • Your phone number
  • Your email address
  • Your business type (optional)
  • Your approximate monthly income (optional)
  • Your monthly ad budget

We do not use cookies for tracking. We do not use analytics scripts. We do not collect any data automatically beyond what your browser sends to any web server (e.g. IP address in server logs, if hosted on a server).

3. Why We Collect It

The information you submit is used solely to:

  • Assess whether we can help your business
  • Contact you by phone or email to discuss your situation

We will never use your details to send you unsolicited marketing emails or add you to any mailing list without your explicit consent.

4. How We Store It

Form submissions are received directly at our email address. We do not store your details in a database or third-party CRM unless you become a client and we discuss that with you separately. Data is kept only as long as necessary to handle your enquiry.

5. Who We Share It With

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party, except where required by law.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are based in the European Union, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@moros.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. External Links

Our website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to read their own policies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at hello@moros.com.
